# First Solo Full Stack Project - bestBnB

This is our clone of AirBnB, created for our App Academy's 24 week immersive bootcamp.

Live Site: http://authmetestdeploy.herokuapp.com/

<img width="950" alt="Screen Shot 2022-12-05 at 9 11 49 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/97054477/205685960-409c0765-8f0a-4783-b888-0753f8bd9635.png">

bestBnB is a full-stack clone of https://www.airbnb.com/ . Users can add, edit, and delete their home, add reviews to other user's Spots, as well as edit and delete their reviews. Additionally, logged in users can booking their next vacation, and cancel those reservations from their "My Bookings" page.
